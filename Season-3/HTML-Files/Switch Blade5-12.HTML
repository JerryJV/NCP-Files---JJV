Background
Adam, born August 19th, 2052 to poor parents. Both parents died when he was 8. Adam ended up in the system, not that it did much for orphaned children, with everything else that was going on in the world.

At 12 Adam struck out on his own, and ended up with in a gang of youths about his age. They broke into abandoned buildings for protection from the weather, and stole what they needed. They all took on 'gang' names, and Adam was knicknamed Blade.

Near his 16th birthday Adam's abilities began to manifest. He realized that he could alter his density to allow him to sneak into places easier. The gang's fortunes began to improve, as his abilities improved.

A couple of years ago, they broke into an old research lab. It had been ransacked, but they did get some good stuff from it. As Adam was moving through the walls to check everything out, he found a hidden vault. It took him quite a bit of time and concentration to finally enter the vault. He almost passed out because he has to hold his breath as he moves through objects. What he found changed his life.

Inside the vault was a suit of some strange material. He was still immaterial as he was looking at it. When he reached out to touch it, it moved with his hand. He felt it. He solidified and started messing around with it. It seemed to be attuned to his abilities, so he took off his clothes and put it on.

It fit like it was made for him. It even had a hood and face mask. Oh yes, he thought, this is going to help us out a lot. He put his clothes on over the suit and started back through the wall of the vault. He was surprised as he ran into something in the wall that stopped him. He gasped and realized that the mask over his face allowed him to breath, even in the middle of concrete and steel.

So, he tried to climb up and over the blockage, and realized that he was able to 'walk on air' in the suit. OH YEA!!!! This is great.

As he exited the wall, he pulled the hood and mask down and inside of his hoodie top.
